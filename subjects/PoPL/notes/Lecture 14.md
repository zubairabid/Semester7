- `|-` is "with the assumption" 
  
  Which is to say: How to evaluate $(+ x 3)$?
  
  With the assumption that ${x \to 5}$, $(+ x 3) \implies 8$
  
  Or, ${x \to 5} |- (+ x 3) \implies 8$

$$
\Gamma_1 \cdot \Gamma_2 (w) = \Gamma_1(w) \\ 
           or = \Gamma_2(w) 
$$
